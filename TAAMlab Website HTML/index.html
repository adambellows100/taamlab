What is
Torah Trope?

Torah trope (טַעֲמֵי הַמִּקְרָא) is the ancient system of musical cantillation marks placed in the Hebrew Bible. Each symbol tells you exactly how to sing the word — its melody, phrasing, and grammatical weight.

TAAMlab allows you to learn at your own pace and style, making it possible for everyone to learn and enjoy this incredible tradition.
